Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

TimeTape is designed to collect as little personal information as possible. Most of the data remains solely on the user's device.

Information gathered

TimeTape does not collect personal information such as name, email address, or location.

Data within the app

Saved cities and preferences are stored locally on the device.

In-app purchases

Purchases are processed by the Apple App Store. TimeTape does not have access to payment information.

Analytics and monitoring

TimeTape does not currently use personalized advertising or ad tracking.
