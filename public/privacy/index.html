Privacy Policy

TimeTape does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

The app works primarily offline. Your selected cities and app preferences are stored locally on your device.

Optional city database updates may be downloaded from static JSON files hosted by SlowDevLabs through Cloudflare.

Purchases are securely processed by Apple through the App Store. TimeTape does not store your payment information.

TimeTape does not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

No account is required to use TimeTape.

For support, contact: slowdevlabs+timetape@gmail.com
