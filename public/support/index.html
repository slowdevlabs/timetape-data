Frequently Asked Questions

Does TimeTape require an account?

No. TimeTape works without creating an account.

Does TimeTape work offline?

Yes. The app works primarily offline.

What does TimeTape Pro unlock?

TimeTape Pro unlocks unlimited cities and future advanced features.

How do I restore purchases?

Open the Settings section inside the app and tap “Restore Purchases.”

Does TimeTape collect personal information?

No. TimeTape does not collect or track personal information.
